Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026

What Poop Dollars stores

Poop Dollars stores your compensation settings, session history, leaderboard preferences, and audio settings on your device so the app can calculate estimated earnings and remember your choices.

What stays private

Private mode keeps your salary, hourly rate, and detailed session history on-device only. The app does not need camera, contacts, location, or notifications for the core experience.

Public mode

If you choose public mode, the app shares only the public handle, avatar, aggregate leaderboard totals, session count, and currency code needed to show your public leaderboard card. Raw salary is not shared.

Third-party services

Poop Dollars does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Subscriptions are processed through the App Store. If you enable public mode, aggregate leaderboard data is synced through Apple CloudKit.

Data deletion

You can clear all local session history in Settings. You can delete your public profile at any time in Settings, which removes your handle, avatar, and leaderboard data from the public database.

Children

Poop Dollars is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact us through the support link on the App Store product page.
