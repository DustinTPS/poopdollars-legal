Support

Need help?

For subscription questions, leaderboard issues, moderation reports, or general support, please contact us using the information below.

What to include

If something looks wrong, please include your public handle (if you have one), the phone model, the iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Subscriptions

Poop Dollars subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel or change your plan, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device, or visit Manage Subscriptions.

Leaderboard safety

You can keep the app fully private, block public profiles locally, remove your own public profile at any time, and report unsafe handles through support.
