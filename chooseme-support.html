ChooseMe Support

Support for the moments that matter.

ChooseMe is built to help people stay grounded through breakup recovery, no-contact streaks, SOS moments, journaling, and gentle reflection. If something in the app is not working the way it should, we want to help.

Contact support

Email dustinsparage@aol.com and include your device model, iOS version, and a short note about what happened.

What we can help with

  • Subscription and restore issues
  • Account, sync, or sign-in trouble
  • Bug reports and app crashes
  • Deletion and privacy requests

Response time

Most support emails receive a response within 2 business days. Urgent billing and account-access issues are prioritized first.

Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the paywall and tap Restore purchases. If the purchase still does not appear, email support and we will help you verify the App Store account tied to the subscription.

Do I need an account to use ChooseMe?

You can begin the core experience without a complicated setup. Some cloud-backed features may require account services so your recovery history can stay with you across devices.

How do I delete my data?

Email support with the subject line ChooseMe Data Deletion. We can guide you through any in-app deletion flow and help confirm removal of synced data where applicable.

Is ChooseMe crisis support?

No. ChooseMe is an emotional support and habit-building app, not crisis care or medical treatment. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health emergency, call or text 988 in the United States or contact your local emergency services.
